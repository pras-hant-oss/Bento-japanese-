Our Story

A Taste of Japan
in the Heart of Leeds

Bento Japanese Street Food & Izakaya is more than a restaurant — it is an experience. Inspired by the bustling street food stalls of Osaka and the intimate charm of Tokyo's neighbourhood izakayas, we bring authentic Japanese flavours to Leeds with every dish we serve.

Our menu celebrates the diversity of Japanese cuisine, from crispy tempura and hand-rolled sushi to soul-warming ramen and sizzling yakitori grilled over charcoal. Every ingredient is chosen with care, every recipe perfected with tradition.

Whether you are sharing small plates with friends over sake, enjoying a hearty bento box for lunch, or slurping a bowl of our signature miso ramen, we promise a dining experience that transports you straight to Japan.

The Experience

An Izakaya Atmosphere,
Right Here in Leeds

Step through our doors and leave the city behind. Our Japan-inspired interiors blend warm wood tones, soft lantern lighting, and traditional decor to create an atmosphere that feels like a hidden gem in the backstreets of Tokyo.

Whether you are here for a quick lunch, a leisurely dinner, or drinks with friends, our easygoing izakaya vibe makes every visit feel special. We also offer a great selection of traditional Japanese drinks to complement your meal.
